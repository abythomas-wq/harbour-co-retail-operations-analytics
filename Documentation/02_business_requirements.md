# Harbour & Co. Retail Operations Analytics

# Business Requirements Document

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the business requirements for the Harbour & Co. Retail Operations Analytics solution.

The requirements have been developed to support Project Horizon, the organisation's omnichannel transformation programme, by identifying the analytical capabilities required to improve operational visibility and decision-making.

---

# 2. Business Objective

Harbour & Co. Retail requires an analytics solution to understand how operational performance is changing as the organisation expands its e-commerce capability.

The solution should enable leadership teams to:

- improve inventory efficiency
- optimise fulfilment operations
- monitor supplier performance
- understand customer and product value
- support evidence-based operational decisions

---

# 3. Business Requirements

---

# BR001 — Inventory Performance Visibility

## Business Need

Harbour & Co. requires improved visibility of inventory performance across stores, warehouses and online operations.

Rapid growth in digital sales has increased complexity in allocating stock effectively across the retail network.

## Business Questions

The organisation needs to understand:

- Which products have excessive inventory levels?
- Which products are at risk of stock shortages?
- Where is inventory positioned across the network?
- Which products have slow sales performance?

## Required Analysis

The solution should provide visibility into:

- inventory levels
- stock movements
- product demand
- inventory ageing
- stock availability

## Business Outcome

Enable improved inventory allocation and reduce inefficient stock holding.

---

# BR002 — Fulfilment Performance Management

## Business Need

Growing online demand has increased pressure on Harbour & Co.'s fulfilment operations.

Leadership requires visibility into the efficiency of order processing and delivery performance.

## Business Questions

The organisation needs to understand:

- Are customer orders being fulfilled within expected timeframes?
- Which warehouses experience operational delays?
- What factors contribute to late deliveries?

## Required Analysis

The solution should analyse:

- order processing time
- warehouse performance
- shipment performance
- delivery completion rates
- fulfilment trends

## Business Outcome

Improve customer experience by identifying operational bottlenecks.

---

# BR003 — Supplier Performance Analysis

## Business Need

Harbour & Co. relies on a large supplier network to support product availability.

The organisation requires greater visibility into supplier reliability.

## Business Questions

The organisation needs to understand:

- Which suppliers consistently meet delivery expectations?
- Which suppliers create operational risks?
- How do supplier delays affect product availability?

## Required Analysis

The solution should provide:

- supplier delivery performance
- purchase order performance
- lead time analysis
- supplier reliability metrics

## Business Outcome

Support better supplier management and procurement decisions.

---

# BR004 — Product and Category Performance

## Business Need

Revenue growth does not automatically indicate profitable growth.

Harbour & Co. requires better understanding of product and category performance.

## Business Questions

The organisation needs to understand:

- Which categories drive revenue?
- Which products perform poorly?
- Which products contribute the most value?

## Required Analysis

The solution should analyse:

- sales performance
- product contribution
- category trends
- customer demand

## Business Outcome

Enable improved commercial decision-making.

---

# BR005 — Customer and Channel Performance

## Business Need

Harbour & Co. operates across multiple sales channels and requires understanding of customer behaviour.

## Business Questions

The organisation needs to understand:

- How do customers interact across channels?
- Which channels generate the most value?
- What behaviours indicate customer loyalty?

## Required Analysis

The solution should analyse:

- customer purchasing behaviour
- channel performance
- repeat purchases
- customer segmentation

## Business Outcome

Support improved customer experience and channel strategy.

# 4. Requirement Prioritisation

| Requirement | Priority | Business Importance |
|---|---|---|
| Inventory Performance | High | Critical for operational efficiency |
| Fulfilment Performance | High | Critical for customer experience |
| Supplier Performance | Medium | Supports supply chain resilience |
| Product Performance | High | Supports commercial decisions |
| Customer Performance | Medium | Supports long-term growth |

# 5. Functional Requirements

The analytics solution should:

| ID | Requirement |
|-|-|
| FR001 | Store operational data in a structured relational model |
| FR002 | Enable analysis of sales and operational performance |
| FR003 | Provide KPI calculations for key business areas |
| FR004 | Support interactive reporting and dashboards |
| FR005 | Enable identification of operational improvement opportunities |
