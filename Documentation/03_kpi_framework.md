# Harbour & Co. Retail Operations Analytics

# KPI Framework

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the key performance indicators (KPIs) required to measure operational performance across Harbour & Co. Retail.

The KPI framework supports Project Horizon by providing consistent measurements across inventory management, fulfilment operations, supplier performance, commercial performance and customer behaviour.

---

# 2. KPI Principles

The KPI framework has been designed using the following principles:

- KPIs must align with strategic business objectives
- Metrics must support decision-making
- Definitions must remain consistent across reporting
- KPIs should identify improvement opportunities rather than only measure performance

---

# 3. Inventory Performance KPIs

## KPI001 — Inventory Turnover

### Definition

Measures how efficiently inventory is converted into sales over a given period.

### Formula
Cost of Goods Sold / Average Inventory Value

### Business Question

"Are we holding the right amount of inventory to support customer demand?"

### Stakeholder

Supply Chain Director

### Target Outcome

Improve working capital efficiency and reduce excess inventory.

---

## KPI002 — Stock Availability Rate

### Definition

Measures the percentage of products available when customers demand them.

### Formula
Available Stock Items / Total Requested Stock Items × 100

### Business Question

"Are customers able to purchase products when required?"

### Stakeholder

Supply Chain Director / Head of E-commerce

---

## KPI003 — Inventory Ageing

### Definition

Measures how long inventory remains unsold.

### Business Question

"Which products are becoming slow-moving stock?"

### Stakeholder

Commercial Director

---

# 4. Fulfilment Performance KPIs

## KPI004 — Order Fulfilment Rate

### Definition

Measures the percentage of customer orders successfully fulfilled.

### Formula
Fulfilled Orders / Total Orders × 100

### Business Question

"Can Harbour & Co. meet customer demand?"

### Stakeholder

Head of E-commerce

---

## KPI005 — Average Order Processing Time

### Definition

Measures the average time between order placement and shipment dispatch.

### Formula
Dispatch Date - Order Date


### Business Question

"How efficiently are warehouses processing orders?"

### Stakeholder

Distribution Centre Managers

---

## KPI006 — On-Time Delivery Rate

### Definition

Measures the percentage of deliveries completed within the expected timeframe.

### Formula
Orders Delivered On Time / Total Delivered Orders × 100

### Business Question

"Are delivery operations meeting customer expectations?"

### Stakeholder

Customer Experience Lead

---

# 5. Supplier Performance KPIs

## KPI007 — Supplier On-Time Delivery Rate

### Definition

Measures supplier reliability based on delivery performance.

### Formula
Orders Received On Time / Total Supplier Orders × 100

### Business Question

"Which suppliers create supply chain risk?"

### Stakeholder

Procurement Director

---

## KPI008 — Supplier Lead Time

### Definition

Measures the average time between purchase order placement and supplier delivery.

### Formula
Delivery Date - Purchase Order Date

### Business Question

"How quickly can suppliers respond to demand?"

### Stakeholder

Procurement Director

---

# 6. Commercial Performance KPIs

## KPI009 — Revenue

### Definition

Total sales generated across all channels.

### Formula
Sum of Sales Value

### Business Question

"How is the business performing commercially?"

### Stakeholder

Commercial Director

---

## KPI010 — Average Order Value (AOV)

### Definition

Measures the average revenue generated per customer order.

### Formula
Total Revenue / Total Orders


### Business Question

"Are customers purchasing higher-value baskets?"

### Stakeholder

Commercial Director

---

## KPI011 — Category Revenue Contribution

### Definition

Measures revenue contribution by product category.

### Business Question

"Which categories drive business growth?"

### Stakeholder

Commercial Director

---

# 7. Customer Performance KPIs

## KPI012 — Customer Retention Rate

### Definition

Measures the percentage of customers who return and make additional purchases.

### Business Question

"Are we building long-term customer relationships?"

### Stakeholder

Customer Experience Lead

---

## KPI013 — Customer Lifetime Value

### Definition

Estimates the total value generated by a customer relationship.

### Business Question

"Which customers generate the greatest long-term value?"

### Stakeholder

Commercial Director

---

# 8. KPI Ownership Matrix

| KPI Area | Primary Owner | Decision Supported |
|---|---|---|
| Inventory | Supply Chain Director | Stock optimisation |
| Fulfilment | Head of E-commerce | Delivery improvement |
| Supplier | Procurement Director | Supplier decisions |
| Commercial | Commercial Director | Product strategy |
| Customer | Customer Experience Lead | Customer retention |

---

# 9. KPI Implementation Considerations

All KPIs will be calculated using validated operational datasets.

KPI definitions will be documented to ensure consistency between SQL analysis and Tableau reporting.

Where required, assumptions will be documented due to the simulated nature of the dataset.
