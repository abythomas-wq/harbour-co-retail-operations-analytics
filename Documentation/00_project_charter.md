2. Organisation Profile

Company Overview
	Harbour & Co. Retail is a premium UK omnichannel retailer specialising in fashion, home, lifestyle and consumer products.
	The company has established itself as a trusted premium retail brand through a nationwide store network and strong customer relationships.
	In response to changing consumer behaviour and increasing competition from digitally advanced retailers, Harbour & Co. has accelerated investment into its e-commerce capabilities and omnichannel operating 	model.

6. Strategic Context
	Digital Transformation Programme
	Background
	Historically, Harbour & Co. built its success through its premium high-street presence.
	However, consumer purchasing behaviour has shifted significantly towards digital channels.
	Competitive pressure from:
	digitally native retailers
	advanced e-commerce platforms
	highly automated fulfilment networks
	has accelerated the need for operational transformation.

Strategic Objective
	Harbour & Co. aims to strengthen its e-commerce operations by creating a scalable omnichannel retail model.
	Key objectives:
		Increase online revenue contribution
		Improve fulfilment capability
		Optimise inventory allocation
		Maintain premium customer experience
		Improve operational profitability

7. Project Approach

	The project will follow a structured business analysis approach:
	Understand business objectives and stakeholder needs
		Define operational challenges and analytical requirements
		Identify required data sources and design a relational data model
		Develop analytical datasets and KPI calculations
		Deliver insights through interactive reporting
		Provide recommendations to support operational improvement

8. Business Problem Statement
	Despite strong commercial performance, Harbour & Co. is experiencing increasing operational complexity.
	The company currently lacks a consolidated analytical view across its retail ecosystem.
	Leadership requires greater visibility into:
	where inventory is positioned
	how efficiently orders are fulfilled
	which suppliers create operational risks
	which products and channels generate sustainable value

9. Project Objective
	The objective of this project is to design an operations analytics solution that enables Harbour & Co. leadership to make informed decisions by providing visibility into operational performance.
	The solution will analyse:
		sales performance
		inventory efficiency
		fulfilment operations
		supplier reliability
		customer behaviour
