# Harbour & Co. Retail Operations Analytics

# Stakeholder Analysis

## 1. Purpose

This document identifies the key stakeholders involved in Project Horizon, Harbour & Co. Retail's omnichannel transformation programme.

Understanding stakeholder objectives, concerns and information requirements ensures that the analytics solution addresses real business needs and supports effective decision-making.

## 3. Stakeholder Analysis

## Chief Operating Officer (COO)

### Responsibility

Provides executive sponsorship for Project Horizon and ensures the transformation programme delivers measurable business value.

### Key Business Concerns

- Is e-commerce growth operationally sustainable?
- Are operational costs increasing faster than revenue?
- Where should investment be prioritised?

### Information Requirements

Requires:

- Executive performance overview
- Revenue and channel performance
- Fulfilment performance
- Inventory efficiency metrics
- Operational improvement opportunities

---

## Supply Chain Director

### Responsibility

Responsible for inventory management, logistics operations and supply chain performance.

### Key Business Concerns

- Stock availability
- Excess inventory
- Warehouse capacity
- Supply chain efficiency

### Information Requirements

Requires:

- Inventory turnover
- Stock availability
- Inventory ageing
- Warehouse performance
- Supplier reliability

---

## Head of E-commerce

### Responsibility

Responsible for digital sales growth and online customer experience.

### Key Business Concerns

- Maintaining customer service levels during growth
- Improving online fulfilment capability
- Reducing delivery-related complaints

### Information Requirements

Requires:

- Online sales trends
- Order fulfilment performance
- Delivery SLA performance
- Returns analysis
- Customer purchasing behaviour

---

## Commercial Director

### Responsibility

Responsible for product strategy, category performance and profitability.

### Key Business Concerns

- Which products drive value?
- Which categories underperform?
- Is revenue growth profitable?

### Information Requirements

Requires:

- Product performance
- Category analysis
- Revenue trends
- Margin analysis
- Customer value insights

---

## Procurement Director

### Responsibility

Responsible for supplier relationships and purchasing strategy.

### Key Business Concerns

- Supplier reliability
- Lead times
- Supply disruption risks

### Information Requirements

Requires:

- Supplier delivery performance
- Supplier quality metrics
- Lead time analysis
- Purchase order performance

---

## Distribution Centre Managers

### Responsibility

Responsible for warehouse operations and fulfilment execution.

### Key Business Concerns

- Processing capacity
- Order delays
- Operational bottlenecks

### Information Requirements

Requires:

- Order processing times
- Warehouse productivity
- Dispatch performance
- Delivery delays
